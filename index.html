📝 Description

Store Update Helper is a lightweight utility designed to help Android users manage application updates, view system information, and access important device settings quickly. The app brings together version details, Google Play Services information, and system shortcuts in one convenient place without collecting personal data.

✨ Key Features

  • View installed app version information
  • Check Google Play Services version
  • Quick access to system update settings
  • App management shortcuts
  • Storage and battery information
  • Network and device details
  • Dark Mode support
  • No advertisements

📱 Compatibility

✓ Android phones running Android 8.0 and above
✓ Android tablets with minimum 7-inch display
✓ Works offline for most features

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Introduction

Store Update Helper is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Our application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app version data and system information displayed within the app is read directly from your device and remains on your device at all times.

3. How Information Is Used

Since we do not collect any data, no information is used, shared, or processed externally. All functionality operates locally on your Android device.

4. Permissions Used

Our app may request the following permissions solely for core functionality:
• QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES – to list installed applications and their versions
• INTERNET – only used when you choose to open the Google Play Store
No permissions are used for tracking or advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. When you tap "Update" for an app, you are redirected to the official Google Play Store, which operates under Google's privacy policy.

6. Advertising

Store Update Helper does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising identifiers.

7. Cookies

This application does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure the app functions securely. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches related to personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any data stored by this application. Since all data remains on your device, you can clear the app data through Android Settings at any time.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
